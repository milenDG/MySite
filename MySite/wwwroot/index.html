Home

I am a student in University of Surrey, where I achieved a score of 90.5 % for the first year of my BSc (Hons) Computer Science course. This summer I had the opportunity to contribute to a military information software for the Bulgarian Codification Bureau of NATO in Technologica Ltd (the largest and most prosperous Bulgarian software company). My main achievements are in the algorithmic core of that specific system. As far as my interests are concerned, Mathematics has always been my favourite science, because I reckon that it is in the base of everything – not only when it comes to algorithms, data-structures, modelling, but also it is an irreplaceable part even in our everyday lives. Therefore, I consider that being a capable software engineer is not possible without a great deal of mathematical knowledge. Moreover, as I have always been one of the best in my class at Maths, I believe that I will have the opportunity to prosper in the sphere of Computer Science.

Experience


Technologica Ltd.

While working at Technologica Ltd. I had the opportunity to be a part of the team developing the military data exchange system of the Bulgarian National Codification Bureau for NATO. My responsibility in the team was to create the core of the system, which was validating, processing, saving the messages and responding to them. At Technologica I met many friendly people, which are still very close friends to me, and found a friendly working environment, encouraging everyone to show the best version of themselves.


Gamesoft Ltd.

When I was working in Gamesoft Ltd. as a software engineer, I gained a great deal of knowledge about the principles of Object-oriented programming and enhanced my coding skills, because writing high-quality and tested programming code was an essential part of my job. While working there my contribution were to write software from previously clarified requirements. Moreover, we were modelling databases and adding procedural code for validation and processing of the data.
